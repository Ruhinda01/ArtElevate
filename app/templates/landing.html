Features

Artwork Showcase

The platform serves as a virtual gallery, offering a curated space to showcase artists' works on the homepage, akin to the walls of an art gallery in the physical world.

User Experience

The platform offers users an immersive experience, allowing them to browse through artwork from various artists, curate their favorite pieces, and support artists by purchasing their artwork.

Checkout

The platform enables users to securely and conveniently checkout their selected artworks.

About

This project was inspired by the need to create a space where artists can showcase their works without being limited by their popularity or affiliation with studios or institutions. The platform aims to democratize the art market, providing opportunities for artists to reach a wider audience and supporting them along their artistic journey. Additionally, it serves as a hub for art lovers and enthusiasts to discover new artists, explore various styles, and uncover potential talents.
